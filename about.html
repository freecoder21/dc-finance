About DCM Financing

Your trusted partner for complex commercial real estate financing

Who we are

DCM Financing is a commercial real estate finance partner for seasoned investors and developers. We live in the details, think creatively, and keep deals moving. Founded with the vision to provide sophisticated capital solutions for complex transactions, we've built our reputation on delivering results where others see obstacles.

How we work

Personal, hands-on, and transparent. You always know where your deal stands and what is next. Our approach is built on direct communication, deep industry knowledge, and a commitment to finding creative solutions that get deals done.

Why clients stay

Our clients return because we deliver exceptional value through:

Very personal service

Direct access to decision-makers and dedicated support throughout your financing journey.

Deep experience with complex structures

Expertise in navigating challenging scenarios and creating innovative financing solutions.

Valuable lender network

Direct relationships with capital sources that understand sophisticated commercial real estate.

Direct communication and clear timelines

Transparent processes with regular updates and realistic expectations from start to finish.

And on top of that, they stay for the results we deliver on every single deal.

Custom capital, not cookie-cutter

We do not push a product. We tailor capital to fit the asset and the business plan. That can include senior debt, construction financing, bridge solutions, takeout strategies, and creative gap coverage when needed. The goal is simple, get you closed on strong terms with clear execution.

How we think

  • Understand the deal story and risks early
  • Map the capital stack to the timeline and exit
  • Target lenders who already like this exact profile
  • Negotiate for speed, certainty, and flexibility
